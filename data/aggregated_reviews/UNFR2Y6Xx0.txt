Aggregated Review:
### Key Points
This paper presents a novel dataset, IDTraffickers, aimed at combating human trafficking by facilitating the identification of vendors through authorship attribution and verification. The authors highlight the challenges of conventional methods that require extensive human intervention on large-scale data. They propose leveraging LLMs and vector stores for initial data processing, followed by human analysis for finer insights. The dataset is substantial, diverse, and includes relevant statistics to demonstrate its quality. The authors outline two tasks: Authorship Identification using a standard LLM + Classifier setup, and Authorship Verification utilizing a vector store.

### Strengths and Weaknesses
Strengths:
- The paper addresses a critical societal issue, providing a unique dataset that can enhance tools for law enforcement.
- The dataset is large enough to fine-tune LLMs, and the reported statistics support its quality.
- The design of the tasks and the methods for classification and recall are innovative and cutting-edge.

Weaknesses:
- Concerns regarding the stability of the dataset over time and the susceptibility of trained models to drift.
- Questions about the reliability of PII-filtering techniques and the ethical implications of data privacy.
- Lack of examples correlating text structure with vendor labels raises doubts about the models' learning processes.

### Suggestions for Improvement
We recommend that the authors improve the discussion on the stability of the dataset over time and its impact on model performance. Additionally, please provide clarity on the reliability of PII-filtering techniques and address ethical concerns more thoroughly in the main text. We suggest including examples of text as they correlate to vendors to demonstrate the models' learning processes. Furthermore, consider providing experimental results to justify the use of multilingual models and discuss the generalization of styles among vendors over time. Lastly, a thorough spelling and grammar check is advised to enhance the overall presentation.
