### Key Points
This paper presents a novel approach for ODE parameter identification based on collocation methods, specifically utilizing an alternating linear regression approach based on LGR. The authors demonstrate through experimental results that their method is more accurate and efficient compared to existing baselines.

### Strengths and Weaknesses
Strengths:  
The paper is well-written and aligns well with the workshop scope, providing a valuable contribution to the community. The proposed method shows improved accuracy and efficiency in ODE parameter identification.

Weaknesses:
